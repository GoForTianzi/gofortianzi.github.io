The Compost Pedallers

Title Design

Overview

This was a short documentary I made about The Compost Pedallers, a company in Austin that provides organic waste collecting service and donates the waste to nearby city farms. I hand made the titles to pay homage to the interviewee and the production team. Materials used include lotus seed buds, onion and orange peels, tea leaves and grains.
